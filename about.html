About Us

Welcome to Dakshin for an unforgettable authentic dining experience.

Dakshin – restaurant formed by two foodies from Hyderabad in search for a true authentic South Indian cuisine. Southern India, Home to the musical elite, and Home to regional rhythms and ragas, Home to the diverse musical notes. Hyderabad a city located on the banks of river Musi in India, known for Charminar, Pearls and Biryani is a one stop place for all food lovers. Biryani is synonymous with Hyderabad yet the subtitle of its cuisine.

At Dakshin we take it a step forward and fuse these musical rhythms of selected regions with a melody of flavors from their respective cuisines. Inspired by the very legacy, Dakshin brings you same of the selective delicious of the bygone era perfecting to its glory..

The restaurant’s decor is as remarkable as its cuisine and has a period feel with a modern twist. Spacious, sensual and soft. Dakshin is perfect for long lunches, full-on feasts or intimate dinners. Focus lighting, turmeric colored walls and in the middle huge water fall instantly put you at ease. An interesting feature is the musical instruments displayed on the walls – all linked to the marvelous regional cuisines. Dakshin provides a relaxed and stylish venue to enjoy the ultimate Indian food. Basically we offer the perfect balance between taste and aesthetics. Private parties and special events can be celebrated here. And at Dakshin diners will have an opportunity to experience and enjoy the finest Indian food with the finest music. The specialty of the restaurant lies in south Indian food and Hyderabadi cuisine. It is conveniently located at Dublin next to Patel Bros, and is a perfect destination for Indian food lovers.

Dakshin–The name itself speaks about the traditional authentic South Indian spicy food. Our dishes are for its rich in aroma, absolutely pure and handpicked spices, serves the best of the variety of Indian mouth watering delicacies. It is well know that Indian food is nothing less than a cultural phenomenon. The legendary Maharajas, Shahenshas, Nawabs and Sultans contributed immensely to the development of music and cuisines in their respective regions and Dakshin offer diners their most fabulous creations.

Our fascinating menu draws from the greatest cooking traditions of North, South, East and West India and features the most popular desi delicacies. The magnificently diverse historic states of Andhra, Tamilnadu, Kerala, & Karnataka are known for their rich musical heritage and cuisines. Having been the cultural capitals during the country’s most glorious eras, each region prides itself with its own distinct style of cooking and we have it all on our menu.

Dakshin is one stop Indian restaurant in Columbus, Ohio known for authentic Indian delicacy. They serve one of the best vegetarian and non vegetarian Hyderabadi Dum Biryani across USA. The menu selection is a gourmet’s delight hailing from the distinct locations, each offering the most authentic and exotic signature dishes. Southern India is the Home of the cuisine features many coastal delights including an enticing variety of fish and prawns. The Hyderabadi royals were famous for luxury and often one dish could be composed of as many as 32 rare spices. The dishes are full of aroma and vibrant in color. Exploding with flavor, Hyderabadi Biryani is perhaps the region’s best known gift.

All the dishes on the menu are cooked with personal care and not to mention their own spices to give you that exceptional taste, and experience straight from Hyderabad, which is hard to find elsewhere.

Dakshin menu features a wide variety of exceptional traditional South Indian vegetarian and non-vegetarian food. Whether it is traditional Hyderabadi Dum Biryani, mouth-watering Tandoori Kebabs or tangy Nellore Fish Pulusu they have it all.

If you are a vegetarian they have, Idly, Vada, Puri, Dosa Dal Tadka, Malai Kofta to tingle your taste buds. That is not all for desert they have Gulab Jaamun, Pine Apple Mastani, Passion Fruit Soufflé , Mango Cheese Cake prepared in an authentic way, which will surely satisfy your sweet tooth.

Not to mention for all the health conscious people, Dakshin is a healthier place to eat because of the fresh made-to-order food prepared with fresh-grounded spices, fresh vegetables and fresh meats, served in an upscale atmosphere.
